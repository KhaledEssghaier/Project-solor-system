Articles Documentaires

Earth is the third planet in the solar system and is unique for its ability to support life, with a rich atmosphere containing oxygen and liquid water.

Mars, known as the Red Planet, is the fourth planet in the solar system, featuring desert-like landscapes, mountains, and polar ice caps, with evidence of past water.

The Moon is Earth's natural satellite, characterized by its cratered surface and its influence on Earth's tides.

Venus is the second planet in the solar system, often referred to as Earth's sister due to its similar size, but it has a dense and toxic atmosphere, leading to extreme temperatures.

Jupiter is the largest planet in the solar system, classified as a gas giant, with an atmosphere primarily composed of hydrogen and helium, and is famous for its Great Red Spot, a massive storm.
